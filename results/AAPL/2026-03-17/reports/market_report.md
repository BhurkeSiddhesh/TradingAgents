# AAPL Technical Analysis Report

## Executive Summary
AAPL has experienced a significant technical breakdown from its December 2025 peak of $285 to current levels around $252. The stock shows clear bearish momentum across multiple timeframes, with the 50-day moving average declining through the 200-day moving average, indicating a potential medium-term downtrend. However, the stock is currently finding support around the $250 level and showing signs of consolidation rather than continued sharp decline.

## Detailed Analysis

### **Trend Analysis**
The moving averages reveal a deteriorating technical picture:
- **50 SMA (262.20)**: Declining steadily from recent highs, acting as dynamic resistance
- **200 SMA (245.46)**: More stable but also declining, providing long-term support around $245
- **10 EMA (257.89)**: Most responsive indicator, currently below both longer-term averages
- **VWMA (261.84)**: Volume-weighted average aligns with price action, confirming bearish sentiment

The relationship between these averages indicates a bearish trend with the short-term 10 EMA leading the decline, followed by the medium-term 50 SMA, while the long-term 200 SMA provides a safety net.

### **Momentum Analysis**
**MACD Indicator:**
- Current value: -2.95 (bearish momentum)
- Recent cross below zero confirms momentum shift
- Shows weakening momentum as the histogram becomes more negative
- The MACD line has been declining since early March, indicating bearish momentum is strengthening

**RSI Indicator:**
- Current value: 38.54 (neutral but bearish)
- Has declined from above 50 in early March to current levels
- Not yet oversold (below 30), suggesting further downside potential
- Shows bearish momentum but not at extreme levels

### **Volatility Analysis**
**ATR (Average True Range):**
- Current value: 5.98 (moderate volatility)
- Has remained relatively stable in the 6.0-6.8 range
- Indicates consistent price movement without extreme volatility spikes
- Suggests the current decline is orderly rather than panic-driven

**Bollinger Bands Middle Line (20 SMA):**
- Current value: 262.60
- Similar to the 50 SMA, acting as a dynamic reference point
- The middle band has been declining, confirming the bearish trend

### **Volume and Price Relationship**
**VWMA (Volume-Weighted Moving Average):**

- Declining from recent highs, confirming price trend

## Trading Strategy Recommendations

### **Bullish Scenarios**
- **Breakout above $265**: Would indicate trend reversal and potential test of $280
- **RSI crosses above 50**: Would confirm momentum shift
- **Price holds above 200 SMA ($245)**: Would indicate healthy consolidation

### **Bearish Scenarios**
- **Break below $245**: Would target $240-$235 levels
- **RSI continues declining**: Could reach oversold territory (30-35) before bottoming
- **50 SMA crosses below 200 SMA**: Would confirm death cross and further downside

### **Risk Management**
- **Stop Loss**: For long positions, place stop below $245 (200 SMA)
- **Position Sizing**: Given current volatility (ATR ~6), adjust position sizes accordingly
- **Target Levels**: For short positions, targets at $245, $240, $235

## Final Assessment
AAPL is in a clear downtrend with multiple technical indicators confirming bearish momentum. The stock has broken below key moving averages and is currently finding support around the $250 level. The indicators suggest the decline may continue towards the $245 level before finding more significant support. Traders should be cautious and wait for clearer reversal signals before establishing long positions.
